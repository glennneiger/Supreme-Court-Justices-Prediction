    Chief Justice Warren E. Burger 
    I have the judgment and the opinion of the court to announce in 74-5968 Geders against the United States, and for reasons stated in an opinion filed with the clerk this morning, the judgment of the court of appeals for the fifth circuit is reversed. 
    The case is remanded to the court of appeals of that circuit with directions that it be remanded in turn to the district court for proceedings consistent with our opinion. 
    Mr. Justice Marshall, with whom Mr. Justice Brennan joined filed a concurring opinion. 
    Mr. Justice Stevens took no part in consideration or decision of the case.
