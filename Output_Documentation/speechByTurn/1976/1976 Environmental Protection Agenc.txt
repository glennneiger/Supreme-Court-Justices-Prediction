    Chief Justice Warren E. Burger 
    I have the disposition to announce in 75-909, Environmental Protection Agency against Governor Brown of California and the three related and consolidated cases and for reasons stated in an opinion for the Court filed with the clerk this morning, the several judgments of the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit and the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit are vacated, and the cases are remanded for consideration of mootness and for such other proceedings as maybe consistent with this opinion.
